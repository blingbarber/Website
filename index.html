Biography

Early Life

Carlo Acutis (Born on 3 May 1991, and died on 12 October 2006) was an English-born Italian Catholic teenager known for his devotion to the Eucharist, and using digital tools to spread his knowledge to many people.

Born in London and raised in Milan, he had an interest in computers and video games, then teaching himself programming and web design. He served as a catechist and helped inspire many people to becoming Catholic.

Miracles

These two miracles happened after prayers asking for Carlo Acutis's help.

I

Firstly, the healing of a Brazilian Boy in 2013: A 3 year old boy in Brazil suffering from a severe pancreatic disease, was somehow healed after praying to Carlo and revieving one of his artifacts.

II

Secondly, A 21 year old Costa Rican student named Valeria Valverde fell off her bike one day and got a severe head injury in Florence, Her mother made a pilgrimage to Carlo's tomb in Assisi, and on the same day, Valeria began showing signs of recovery, with scans showing her injuries had been healed.
